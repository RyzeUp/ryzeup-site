Web Dev Project Landing Page

Here is our project RyzeUp. Intended to show the connections and networks in the political system of the US, it displays information in a easy to see, interactive, and fluid way. Allows for users to go through and see their representatives, look at bills, read user explanations and comments, and see what their representative is actually a part of.
